## 🎨 Visual Design

### Color System
- 🔴 **Red:** High risk, overdue, concerns
- 🟡 **Yellow:** Medium risk, upcoming deadlines, flags
- 🟢 **Green:** Low risk, safe timelines, approvals
- 🔵 **Blue:** Questions, information, neutral

### Iconography
- 📊 Health Score: Circular gauge
